## Project Overview

This was one of my early engineering projects, built during my undergraduate studies at the University of Michigan.

## Technical Details

The synchronizer was designed to:
1. Align electromagnetic signals across multiple channels
2. Reduce phase drift in communication systems  
3. Provide stable timing for data transmission

## Design Approach

- Used inductors and capacitors for signal processing
- Implemented analog feedback for automatic adjustment
- Achieved sub-millisecond synchronization accuracy

## Skills Demonstrated

- Circuit design and analysis
- Electromagnetic field theory
- Practical engineering implementation
- Problem-solving with limited resources
